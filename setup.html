Virtual Try-On Setup

Welcome to Virtual Try-On! To get started, please upload 1-2 photos of yourself that will be used for trying on garments.

For best results:

  • Use full-body photos where you're standing straight
  • Use photos with good lighting and a neutral background
  • Wear fitted, simple clothing (preferably solid colors)

Your photos will be stored locally on your device and are only sent to our AI model when you choose to try on a garment.
